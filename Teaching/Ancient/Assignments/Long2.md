### Prompt 

In *Phys.* II. Ch. 3, Aristotle distinguishes 4 different causes. First explain each of these causes. Then explain how Aristotle would use these four causes to explain the growth of an oak tree from a sapling into an adult. Suggest candidates for the four causes of this growth and explain how these causes differ from one another.
