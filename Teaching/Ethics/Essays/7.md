## Book 6

Aristotle distinguishes practical wisdom from craft. Write a portrait of a person with craft and a person with practical wisdom. Use your two portraits to identify the precise difference between practical wisdom and craft. 

Make sure in this paper to clarify how happiness is supposedly an action for Aristotle and not a product of an action.
